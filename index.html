About me

My name is Tamanna Afroj but generally called by everyone as Mim. I am 26 years old.I am a dedicated software engineer with a strong academic foundation in computer science and a passion for building innovative digital solutions. Growing up in a small town, I was always fascinated by how technology could connect people and solve real-world problems. This curiosity led me to pursue programming, starting with basic HTML and CSS in school and gradually advancing to complex frameworks like React and Node.js during my university years. My journey as a developer has been shaped by diverse experiences, from contributing to open-source projects to developing web applications for local businesses. I specialize in front-end development, with a knack for creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces. Whether it's building a personal portfolio site, an e-commerce platform, or data-driven dashboards, I thrive on turning ideas into interactive experiences. Over the years, I’ve also explored the world of machine learning and neural networks, applying these skills to small-scale projects focused on predictive analytics and pattern recognition. My technical toolkit includes JavaScript, React, Python, and MySQL, with a constant drive to expand my knowledge and stay updated with emerging technologies.In addition to coding, I’m an advocate for continuous learning and collaboration. I enjoy mentoring junior developers, participating in hackathons, and engaging with developer communities. My ultimate goal is to contribute to projects that leave a meaningful impact, whether it’s through efficient software solutions or innovative tech products. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring creative hobbies like digital design, brainstorming startup ideas, or simply unwinding with a good book. Life, to me, is about staying curious and creating something valuable with every step of the journey.
